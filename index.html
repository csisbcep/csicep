About CSI

The Computer Society of India (CSI) is the largest and most professionally managed association of and for IT professionals in India. The purposes of the Society are scientific and educational directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of computer science and IT.

Our Chapter

Vision

To empower students with technical excellence and foster a culture of continuous innovation.

Mission

Bridging the gap between industry and academia through workshops, hackathons, and mentorship.

Activities

Regular coding competitions, expert talks, and hands-on sessions on emerging technologies.
